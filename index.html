Switch Access for webpages

Give AAC/switch users switch/keyboard navigation on your webpage with highlighing and other neat functions

If you have a webpage where you want to provide accessability with switch control. This library is for you. As most webpages are required to be accessible for all users, it's highly likely that you need to implement this kind of functionality
